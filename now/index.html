Now

I mainly:

  • Program.
  • Workout.
  • Dance tango.
  • Sleep.

During the week I walk to the Soleil Synchrotron, France, where I am a post-doc. I am trying to extend Orange, a visual programming toolkit for data analysis, so that it will handle spectral data and perhaps also data from tomography. We call our project Quasar.

If tango does not make me too tired, I follow the GMB Integral Strength workout program. Every day I hang for 7 minutes. I started a week ago and it is already becoming easier. When feeling courageous, I take a cold shower.

I go to afternoon milonga on Sunday (Dolce vita) regularly. Sometimes I visit the Friday milonga (Oh la la), but there I leave among the first. I avoid staying up late. We practice at home a lot.

— January 2019
